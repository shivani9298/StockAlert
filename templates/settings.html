Setup Help

  1. Enable 2-Factor Authentication in your Google Account
  2. Go to Google Account → Security → App passwords
  3. Generate an App Password for "Mail"
  4. Use that 16-character password in the "Email App Password" field
  5. Use your regular Gmail address in "Your Email Address"

Buy Alert: Use negative values (e.g., -2.0). You'll get alerted when stocks drop by this percentage or more.

Sell Alert: Use positive values (e.g., 3.0). You'll get alerted when stocks rise by this percentage or more.

Example: With -2.0% buy and 3.0% sell, you'll be notified when stocks drop 2% (good buying opportunity) or rise 3% (consider taking profits).
